Gordon James Ramsay

Celebrity Chef

Gordon Ramsay is a celebrity chef who owns multiple restaurants, hosts television shows such as MasterChef and Hell's Kitchen, and has written over 20 books. Born in Scotland, he quit an early sporting career to become a well-known chef in London. By the early 2000s, he had created a name for himself on British television as the volatile host of Ramsay's Kitchen Nightmares and Hell's Kitchen, which had a successful run in the United States. Since then, the award-winning chef has developed his celebrity brand through programs like MasterChef and Next Level Chef, as well as built new restaurants throughout the world.

Arnold Poernomo

Celebrity Chef

The route to one's heart is through the stomach, but having a nice face doesn't hurt either. Arnold gained popularity with his cooking show Catatan Chef Arnold (Chef Arnold Notes), as well as his participation on MasterChef Indonesia, first as a guest chef in season two and later as a judge in seasons three and four. He then piqued the interest of his wife Tiffany Soetanto at his restaurant NOMZ, who requested if she could take him away. Born and raised in a culinary-savvy family with two brothers, Arnold's masterpieces are based on self-learning and a passion for the food industry.

Chef Wan

Celebrity Chef

Chef Wan, whose real name is Datuk Redzuawan Ismail, is a renowned Malaysian celebrity chef, restaurateur, and television personality celebrated for his vibrant personality and efforts to promote Malaysian cuisine globally. Trained at prestigious culinary institutions like the California Culinary Academy, he has hosted numerous cooking shows, authored cookbooks, and won awards such as Best Celebrity Chef in the World at the Gourmand World Cookbook Awards. Honored with the title of "Datuk" for his contributions to the culinary arts, Chef Wan is a passionate advocate for Malaysia's food heritage and culture.

Chef Anis Nabilah

Celebrity Chef

Chef Anis Nabilah is a renowned Malaysian celebrity chef and television host known for her dynamic culinary skills and engaging personality. A graduate of the Food Institute of Malaysia, she specializes in combining traditional Malaysian flavors with modern cooking techniques. Anis has hosted numerous cooking shows, including international productions, and is a regular face on Malaysian television. Passionate about promoting Malaysian cuisine globally, she has also appeared at food festivals and events around the world. Her approachable style and creativity in the kitchen have earned her a strong following among food enthusiasts.

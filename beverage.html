TEH TARIK KAW

This refreshing beverage is made with freshly brewed tea, fragrant spices, and milk. Perfect for any time of day!

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces of kaw tea bags
  • 2 tablespoons of mosque-branded fragrant tea
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons vanilla essence
  • 1 1/2 cans of liquid milk
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • salt
  • 6-8 cloves

Step:

  1. In a medium-sized pot, heat the water and vanilla essence.
  2. Then, add the kaw tea bags and mosque-branded fragrant tea, and bring to a boil until the water is both warm and boiling.
  3. Stir in the evaporated milk and sugar syrup, then add the condensed milk to achieve the desired sweetness level.
  4. Cook tea till it bubbles, stirring occasionally. Add salt and cloves for a fatty flavour. Reduce the heat for slower cooking.

PEANUT BUTTER BANANA SMOOTHIE

This peanut butter banana smoothie is great for any meal of the day, including breakfast, lunch, dessert, and snacks.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • Â½ cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Step:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.

  • Ice cube
  • Cold milk

Step:

  2. Start whipping the ingredients until they reach a fluffy consistency and light bit of water.
  3. Fill balf of your favourite cup with ice and about 2/3 with cold milk.

JUICE AVOCADO

A creamy, buttery drink that can be made in under ten minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Avocado ripe
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar or honey

Step:

  1. Slice the avocado in half lengthwise. To release the pulp from the pit, twist the fruit. Scoop out the meat using a spoon after removing the pit.
  2. Put the avocado flesh in a blender. Put the sweetener in. Blend thoroughly after adding half a cup of milk. Blend one more after adding the remaining milk. This guarantees a creamy, rich mouthfeel. You can experiment with adding a few ice cubes.
  3. Serve chilled and enjoy!

WATERMELON SODA

A refreshing and fizzy drink made with fresh watermelon juice and soda.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups watermelon juice
  • 1 cup soda water
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Step:

  1. In a pitcher, combine watermelon juice, soda water, and lime juice. Stir well.
  2. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the watermelon soda mixture over the ice.
  3. Garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.
  4. Enjoy!

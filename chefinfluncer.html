INFLUENCER CHEF



Khairul Aming

TikTok Influencer & Founder of Sambal Nyet & Dendeng Nyet

Khairul Aming, also called Khairul Amin Kamarulzaman, is a well-known Malaysian influential chef and entrepreneur. He rose to prominence during Ramadan with his "30 Hari 30 Resepi" (30 Days 30 Recipes) series, in which he shared a new recipe every day. His Instagram account has almost 3.7 million followers, thanks to his engaging and simple culinary videos.





Che Sayang

TikTok Influencer & Content Creator

Che Sayang, whose real name is Qayyum Halid, is a popular Malaysian social media influencer and content creator known for his culinary videos. He gained recognition through his "Che Sayang Kitchen" on TikTok, where he shares a variety of recipes and cooking tips. His engaging and approachable style has earned him a large following and numerous accolades, including the Creator of the Year award at the TikTok Awards Malaysia 2024.





Che Nom

TikTok Influencer & Content Creator

Che Nom, a Malaysian YouTuber and recipe content creator, shares her culinary journey through her channel, Resepi Che Nom. Born Normah Bakar, she aims to inspire home cooks and small business owners to create authentic Malaysian dishes, with over 2.23 million subscribers and 382 million views on YouTube.





Hamdan Mubarak

TikTok Influencer & Content Creator

Hamdan Mubarak, a Malaysian chef and social media personality, has gained popularity with his TikTok videos. His signature style, "Bismillah," means "In the name of God," adds a personal touch to his content. He shares a variety of recipes, from traditional Malaysian dishes to creative, easy-to-make options. His charismatic personality and sense of humor have earned him a large following on TikTok. His mission is to make cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
